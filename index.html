About our product

Thick, light, and sweet, coating the mouth with tender and bouncy texture. Each drop carries the essence of locally sourced ingredients, carefully selected, and packed with rich flavours. Made with love, crafted for joy. Every sip? Pure heaven.

Our products

Citrus Zest

Bright, refreshing, and irresistibly smooth. Citrus Zest is the perfect fusion of creamy milk and zesty orange. Infused with delicious, chewy orange jelly, every sip bursts with a balance of sweetness and tanginess that will awaken your taste buds. Best enjoyed chilled, it’s a vibrant drink made to keep you feeling fresh, energized, and happy throughout the day.

Grapevine Jelly

Smooth, elegant, and full of flavor. Grapevine Jelly combines the richness of grapes with the creaminess of fresh milk. With the added texture of chewy grape jelly, this drink is both indulgent and refreshing, making it the perfect drink for any time of day. Serve it cold for the ultimate experience of fruity luxury in every sip.

Pink Velvet

Sweet, playful, and velvety smooth. Pink Velvet blends the luscious flavor of ripe strawberries with silky, fresh milk. Enhanced with chewy strawberry jelly, it offers a fun texture and a delightful burst of fruity sweetness. Chill it, sip it, and enjoy a drink that’s as joyful and vibrant as it looks.

Cocoa Dream





Rich, creamy, and full of chocolatey goodness. Cocoa Dream blends fresh milk with the sweetness of chocolate, chewy bite of chocolate jelly. It’s a comforting yet refreshing drink that can be enjoyed anytime whether you’re starting your day, taking a break, or simply craving something sweet. Best served chilled for the ultimate cocoa indulgence.

Mango Tango

Bright, fruity, and irresistibly smooth. Mango Tango combines the natural sweetness of ripe mango with creamy milk, topped off with chewy mango jelly for an exciting twist. Every sip feels tropical, refreshing, and uplifting perfect for cooling down on a hot day or bringing a little sunshine to any moment. Serve it cold and let the flavor dance on your taste buds.
